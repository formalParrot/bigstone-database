Privacy Policy

This project stores the following personal data:

  • Username
  • Password hash (bcrypt, never stored in plain text)
  • Your projects and components tied to your account

Purpose

We use this data only to provide account access and project/component functionality.

Data Retention

Your data is kept until you delete your account. Once deleted, all associated data* is permanently removed.

Your Rights

  • Export your data via GET /users/me/export
  • Delete your account via DELETE /users/me
  • Upload files via this form and contact _justParrot on Discord

Third Parties

We use GitHub Pages (frontend) and Cloudflare D1 & Workers (database & backend). They act as processors.

Contact

DM _justParrot on Discord for data-related questions.

* “Associated data” does not include files. You will get reminded about that when you delete your account. Please, contact _justParrot to remove all associated files, but YOU have to keep the project_id / component_id to get the data removed easily. To get the data, you can export all your account data through our web portal.
