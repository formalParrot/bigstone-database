Privacy Policy

This project stores the following personal data:

  • Username
  • Password hash (bcrypt, never stored in plain text)
  • Your projects and components tied to your account

Purpose: We use this data only to provide account access and project/component functionality.

Data retention: Your data is kept until you choose to delete your account. Once deleted, all associated data is permanently removed.

Your rights:

  • You can request a copy of all your personal data via GET /users/me/export.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data via DELETE /users/me.

Third parties: We use GitHub Pages to host the frontend and Cloudflare D1 to store the database. They act as processors; we remain responsible for compliance.

Contact: For questions regarding your data, please reach out via DM-ing `_justParrot` on discord.
